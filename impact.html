Cultural Impact of JoJo

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure has had a significant impact on pop culture, influencing numerous anime, video games, and even fashion.

  • Inspired many anime series with its unique storytelling
  • Referenced in games like Persona and Street Fighter
  • Memes like "It was me, Dio!" and "Za Warudo!" became internet legends
