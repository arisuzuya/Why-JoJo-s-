What is JoJo's Bizarre Adventure?

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure is a legendary anime and manga series created by Hirohiko Araki. It spans multiple generations, each with unique characters and supernatural battles.

Reasons to watch JoJo:

  • Unique art style and storytelling
  • Memorable characters and battles
  • Influence on pop culture
