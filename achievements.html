JoJo's Bizarre Achievements

JoJo's Bizarre Adventure has achieved remarkable milestones in both the manga and anime industries. Here are some of its most notable accomplishments.

JoJo Art in the Louvre

Hirohiko Araki’s artwork was displayed in the Louvre Museum, making JoJo one of the few manga series to achieve such a prestigious honor.

Worldwide Popularity

JoJo has gained a massive global following, with fans across the world engaging in cosplay, fan art, and discussions.

Innovative Art Style

Araki’s evolving art style has been praised by artists worldwide and has even influenced the fashion industry.

Long-Running Legacy

With over three decades of storytelling, JoJo remains one of the longest-running and most beloved manga series of all time.
